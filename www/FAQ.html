FAQs

  • How do I download the app?
    To start using Helios simply download the Helios app on Play Store for Android devices or App Store for iOS devices.

  • How do I find a Helios Bus Station?
    To find a Helios Bus Station near you, simply open the Helios app. Youâ€™ll automatically be taken to the map screen, showing all available Smart Bus Stations in your area.

  • How do I charge my device?
    You will need to open the QR scanner in the drop down present at the left side (three horizontal lines) and scan the QR code for the wanted USB type (A,B or C). Once you scan it, the USB ports unlock and you can charge your device.

  • How do I stop charging?
    Once the user is done charging, he needs to go the settings and then select "end charging". A page will be displayed with the minutes charged and the money deducted from the wallet. The user can then log out.
